I was in the Alpha Gamma Delta sorority for three years. It pushed me to be way more social and outgoing. üèà Attended lots of Cyclone football games.

Each year, I made sure to go to as many ISU football games as I could. I loved going to the tailgates with everyone! The fall is my favorite time of year so I always loved dressing up in red and yellow with all of my friends and cheering on the Cyclones to victory. üìö Made PR friends and advanced my career.

As I got older, I became more involved outside of greek life. I joined the student organization, Winterfest, in 2022 where I met one of my closest friends, Abby. We instantly hit it off. It was so awesome to create such a wonderful event where we could explore ways to get creative with the activities hosted at the Memorial Union while promoting it on social media. My favorite memory was ice skating with all of the staff members at the end of the night after trying to make Winterfest a success. I also met so many wonderful people in my junior year PR 424 class. We learned how to create an IMC Plan and presented our work where we got first place! It felt less like a team of people and more like actual friends with great memories. It was truly an educational, resume building time that I would not trade for the world!
